In the EU/UK these load only after you tap Accept on the consent bar; Decline keeps this device analytics-free permanently. Elsewhere they're on by default and disclosed here and in the Privacy Policy.

What we don't use

No advertising cookies, no cross-site trackers, no fingerprinting, nothing sold or shared for marketing.

Managing cookies

You can clear or block cookies in your browser settings at any time. Blocking the strictly-necessary ones will sign you out or forget your preferences; blocking analytics changes nothing about how the app works.
