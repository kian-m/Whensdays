Whensdays - plans, minus the group-chat chaos

A free scheduling app for RECURRING friend groups and clubs - book clubs, run clubs, game nights, jams. One link finds when the whole group is free, locks the winning dates as a series, and keeps the streak alive; members vote and RSVP with no app and no account.

How it works

  • Availability polls: exact times, this week, this month, or a general day-and-time grid
  • Schedule one date or several at once as a recurring series
  • Groups with streaks, re-polls, recaps, and reminders
  • Calendar sync (Google and Apple) and one-tap RSVP straight from email
