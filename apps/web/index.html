Whensdays - plans, minus the group-chat chaos

A free scheduling and availability app for friend groups and clubs. Pick a time or let everyone vote on when they're free - guests RSVP from one invite link with no account needed.

  • Availability polls: exact times, this week, this month, or a general day-and-time grid
  • Schedule one date or several at once as a recurring series
  • Groups with streaks, re-polls, recaps, and reminders
  • Calendar sync (Google and Apple) and one-tap RSVP straight from email

Free alternative to Doodle, When2meet, and LettuceMeet for recurring get-togethers - game nights, book clubs, practices, monthly dinners.
